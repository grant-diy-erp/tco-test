Strategic cost — what you can't fully quantify but must name
CategoryDescription
AI capability gapModern ERP platforms (including Business Central) ship Copilot natively. Legacy systems have no AI integration pathway. Competitors adopting AI-assisted forecasting, procurement, and reporting gain a compounding productivity advantage estimated at 10–15% of finance staff cost annually (Gartner, 2024).
Data quality & reportingDisparate systems produce reconciliation overhead and reporting latency. Boards make decisions on data that is days or weeks old. The cost is not captured in spreadsheet hours — it is in decisions made with incomplete information.
Talent attraction & retentionFinance and operations professionals benchmark employers on technology quality. Legacy systems are a friction factor in attracting mid-career talent and a retention risk with high performers who have modern-system experience.
End-of-life support riskVendors routinely discontinue support for older ERP versions. Forced migration costs are typically 30–50% higher than planned migration costs — driven by urgency, reduced partner availability, and accumulated data complexity.
Cyber security exposureOn-premise and legacy systems rely on the organisation's own patching discipline. Cloud-native platforms receive continuous security updates from the vendor. A single breach event at $100M+ revenue typically costs $500k–$2M in remediation, notification, and reputational damage.
